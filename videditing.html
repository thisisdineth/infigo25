VIDEO EDITING

Project Name: CineMorph
Event Type: Team
Regsration Deadline: Dec 10, 2025
OVERVIEW

Task

Make a trailer for a short film.

STRUCTURE

Participation Guidelines

  • Open to students from Grade 6-13.
  • The team shall consist of 3 members.
  • Only one submission per school is accepted.

Design Guidelines

  • Utilize any video editing software of your choice to create the final output, including but not limited to Adobe Premiere Pro, Davinci Resolve, Final Cut Pro, etc.
  • The participants must only use the given video assets while the use of copyright-free audio tracks and voiceovers are accepted.
  • The use of AI tools is strictly prohibited; any submission found using such tools will be disqualified immediately.
  • Your submissions must not include any copyrighted content; violation will result in immediate disqualification of the team and their submission.
  • Videos that deviate from the competition theme will be disqualified.
  • Plagiarism is not allowed.

Submission Guidelines

  • Upload your files to the Google Drive folder provided to you via email.
  • Submit a .zip or .rar file containing all the project files
  • File Naming Format: [Event][SchoolName][YourName].rar
  • If you can’t find your folder link, contact us, and we’ll resend it.
