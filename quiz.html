QUIZ

Project Name: IQnnect
Event Type: Team
Regsration Deadline: Dec 10, 2025
OVERVIEW

Structure of the Competition

  • The Competition is only open to students from Grades 10 to 13.
  • The inter-school quiz competition commences as a team event, in which a team must consist of 4 students and one team per school.
  • The competition will consist of 3 rounds. The first 2 rounds will commence online and the final round will take place during the ICT Day.
  • The determination of the teams advancing to the 2nd round will be based on the number of teams participating in the 1 st round. The top 3 teams from the 2nd round will advance to the finals at INFIGO ’25.

Round 1: Digital Face-Off (Online)

  • Platform: Hosted on the Infigo’25 official platform.
  • Format: 35 multiple-choice questions.
  • Time Limit: 45 minutes.
  • Scoring Criteria: Correct answers + Speed bonus for quick responses.

Round 2: The Tactical Challenge (Online)

  • Platform: Hosted on the Infigo’25 official platform.
  • Format: 40 multiple-choice questions.
  • Time Limit: 45 minutes.
  • Scoring Criteria: Correct answers + Speed bonus for quick responses.
  • Advancement: The top 3 teams will secure their spots in the grand final.

Final Round: The Ultimate Showdown (Physical)

  • Venue: The final battle will be held live at Infigo’25 at Mahanama College.
  • Winner Selection: The team with the highest cumulative score wins the Infigo'25 Championship .
RULES

General Rules

  • All online rounds will take place via the Infigo’25 platform .
  • Teams must ensure a stable internet connection to avoid disruptions.
  • Participants must use their own devices with working cameras and microphones.
  • Any attempt to cheat (external help, multiple logins, unauthorized assistance) will result in instant disqualification.
  • Each team will be assigned to a Zoom Breakout Room for private discussions.

Technical Requirements

  • Device Requirement: The team member responsible for answering (the screen sharer) must use a laptop or a PC. Mobile phones or tablets are not allowed for screen sharing.
  • Stable Internet: Teams must have a reliable internet connection to avoid disruptions.
  • Zoom Access: All team members must be able to join a Zoom meeting for monitoring purposes.
  • Screen Sharing: The designated screen sharer must keep their screen shared throughout the quiz.
  • Camera & Microphone: All participants must have their cameras on and microphones ready for communication when required.
  • No External Assistance: Teams must work independently, and any use of external resources or unauthorized help will lead to immediate disqualification.
