WEB DESIGN

Project Name: CodeCraft
Event Type: Individual
Regsration Deadline: Dec 10, 2025
OVERVIEW

Theme

“ A 3D Animation Studio is a creative production facility dedicated to the development and production of threedimensional animated content. These studios leverage advanced computer-generated imagery (CGI) techniques to bring characters, environments, and stories to life in a visually compelling and immersive manner. These studios typically create animations for various industries, including film, television, advertising, gaming, virtual reality, and more. In these facilities. artistic vision, technical expertise, and advanced technology play a crucial role in producing visually stunning and emotionally compelling animated content across various mediums. “

Task

Make a Web Site for a 3D Animation Studio

STRUCTURE

Participation Guidelines

  • Open to students from Grade 6-13.
  • Individual participation only.
  • No restrictions on the number of submissions per school.
  • Each contestant may submit only one Artwork.

Design Guidelines

  • Participants have full freedom to use their creativity and make a website from a unique idea. The website should align with the title.
  • We encourage participants to think outside the box and come up with innovative, original, and visually striking designs for the website.
  • The website should be clean, and easily recognizable.
  • Feel free to explore different colour palettes and typography options to create a visually appealing composition.
  • 4 to 10 web pages will be appropriate in the final design. Essential pages can be classified as homepage, about us, contact and services.
  • Your website should be responsive. Participants should ensure that their designs are optimized for various devices, including desktops, tablets, and mobile phones. Responsiveness of your design will be considered when distributing marks.
  • Your website should cater a positive user experience.

Submission Guidelines

  • Upload your files to the Google Drive folder provided to you via email.
  • Submit a .zip or .rar file containing all the project files
  • File Naming Format: [Event][SchoolName][YourName].rar
  • If you can’t find your folder link, contact us, and we’ll resend it.
