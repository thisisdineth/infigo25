Project Name: GameStorm
Event Type: Team
Regsration Deadline: Dec 10, 2025
OVERVIEW

Structure of the Competition

  • Eligibility: The competition is only open to students from Grades 10 to 13.
  • Team Composition: This competition is a team event. A team must consist of 5 members, including the leader.
  • School Participation: Only one team can participate per school.
  • Game: Valorant will serve as the featured game for the tournament.
  • Tournament Format: The tournament will proceed in a Single Elimination format, where the losing team of any match will be eliminated upon their first loss.
  • Awards: Recognition and awards will be given to the champion as well as the runner-up.

Competition Details

  • Registration: The In-Game Names (IGNs) & the Riot IDs of the participants must be provided in the registration.
  • Match Pairing: The teams will be matched according to the order theyâ€™ve registered.
  • Match Timing: The matches will be held in the evenings, with one match per day.
RULES

Participation Guidelines

  • Communication: All players are required to join the dedicated Discord server and WhatsApp groups for regular updates and communication.
  • Match Preparation: All players must be online at least 15 minutes before the match starts.
  • Team Availability: If a team fails to gather all their players within 20 minutes of the scheduled match time, the team will be disqualified.
  • Withdrawal Policy: If a team decides not to participate after registering, they must inform the organizing committee at least 2 days before the match.
  • Staying Informed: All players must stay updated on match schedules and rule modifications through WhatsApp and Discord.
  • Fair Play: Cheating and hacking are strictly prohibited during the competition.
  • Consequences for Violations: Any breach of fair play rules will result in penalties, ranging from warnings to disqualification or banning, as deemed appropriate by the organizers.
  • Technical Requirements: All players must ensure that their gaming setup meets the specified technical requirements.
  • System Verification: Participants must verify that their system meets the minimum specifications, including internet speed and other necessary technical requirements.
  • Organizer Responsibility: The organizing committee of INFIGO '25 will not be responsible for any technical faults on the participants' end.
