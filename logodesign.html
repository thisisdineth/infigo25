LOGO DESIGN

Project Name: BrandForge
Event Type: Individual
Regsration Deadline: Dec 10, 2025
OVERVIEW

Theme

“ HoloFusion is a cutting-edge AR/VR technology company dedicated to revolutionising immersive experiences. Leveraging augmented and virtual reality, HoloFusion creates innovative solutions that seamlessly blend the digital and physical worlds. From interactive educational applications to captivating entertainment experiences, HoloFusion is at the forefront of transforming how we perceive and interact with our surroundings. Their expertise lies in crafting immersive content and applications that push the boundaries of reality, offering users a new dimension of engagement and exploration. “

Task

Create a logo for the AR/ VR company mentioned above; HoloFusion

STRUCTURE

Participation Guidelines

  • Open to students from Grade 6-13.
  • Individual participation only.
  • No restrictions on the number of submissions per school.
  • Each contestant may submit only one logo.

Design Guidelines

  • You must use Adobe Illustrator or Adobe Photoshop to create the logo.
  • The design must be original—using templates or copying existing logos will result in immediate disqualification.
  • The logo should be scalable and versatile, suitable for use on both small and large formats.
  • The use of AI tools is strictly prohibited; any submission found using such tools will be disqualified immediately.
  • Color & Monochrome Versions: Submit both a full-color and a black-and-white version.
  • Designs that deviate from the competition theme will be disqualified.
  • File Formats Required:
    • Vector format (.AI or .EPS) for scalability.
    • High-resolution PNG or JPG (300dpi minimum) for presentation.
    • A short PDF document explaining the design concept (symbolism, typography, and color choices).

Submission Guidelines

  • Upload your files to the Google Drive folder provided to you via email.
  • Submit a .zip or .rar file containing all the project files
  • File Naming Format: [Event][SchoolName][YourName].rar
  • If you can’t find your folder link, contact us, and we’ll resend it.
