3D DESIGN

Project Name: TriForma
Event Type: Individual
Regsration Deadline: Dec 10, 2025
OVERVIEW

Task

Design a working environment of a 3D Designer modifying the given assets.

STRUCTURE

Participation Guidelines

  • Open to students from Grade 6-13.
  • Individual participation only.
  • No restrictions on the number of submissions per school.
  • Each contestant may submit only one Artwork.

Design Guidelines

  • Utilize any design software of your choice to create the final output.
  • Participants should recreate the scene within a theme of their choosing and in a preferred art style.
  • The provided starter template and assets must be used as a foundation. Participants are allowed to modify the starter assets at their discretion to achieve the desired creative outcome.
  • The artwork must be original—using templates or copying existing renders will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Participants must adhere strictly to the specified camera positioning and are not permitted to make any changes.
  • Adjustments to render composition settings are allowed to enhance the visual impact of the scene.
  • Use of excessive third-party 3D models will result in a significant deduction in marks.
  • Submissions will be evaluated based on technical competence, emphasizing skill in execution, lighting skills and creativity.
  • The use of AI tools is strictly prohibited; any submission found using such tools will be disqualified immediately.
  • Designs that deviate from the competition theme will be disqualified.
  • File Formats Required:
    • Complete Project files (.blend .c4d etc)
    • High-resolution PNG or JPG of the final render.
    • A short PDF document explaining the design concept (symbolism, typography, and color choices). (optional but may enhance your chances of earning additional points.)

Submission Guidelines

  • Upload your files to the Google Drive folder provided to you via email.
  • Submit a .zip or .rar file containing all the project files
  • File Naming Format: [Event][SchoolName][YourName].rar
  • If you can’t find your folder link, contact us, and we’ll resend it.
