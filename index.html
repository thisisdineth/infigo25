About

The ICT Society of Mahanama College (MCICTS) was founded on 29th September 2012 to uplift the ICT standards of Mahanamians and guide them towards global excellence. Over the years, the society has grown into a leading force in the Sri Lankan school ICT community, organizing impactful events, winning inter-school to national level competitions, and inspiring students to shine in the digital world. Built on dedication, teamwork, and passion, MCICTS continues its legacy — where each generation of brothers carries the torch forward, united and determined to shape the future of ICT.
